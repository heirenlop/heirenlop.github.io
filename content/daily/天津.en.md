            <h2>Introduction</h2>
            <p>2018-02-28 / 2026-03-28 - ongoing</p>
            <p>My first trip to Tianjin was in 2018 for an exam. Later, I started a joint training program at a lab in Beijing, while my girlfriend was pursuing her PhD in Tianjin. Since 2025, I’ve been making regular trips from Beijing to Tianjin. In terms of urban development, Tianjin feels better planned and built than Beijing—probably the best in northern China. The air quality, though, is clearly worse than Beijing’s. And somehow, every time I come here, I never seem to catch a bright sunny day....</p>

    <h2>3. Water Park</h2>
    <p>2026-04-12 <i class="fas fa-cloud"></i></p>
    <p>My girlfriend’s PhD advisor runs a counseling practice in Water Park, so she knows the area pretty well. We came over to stroll around a bit, and since David Tao’s concert was nearby that night, we headed straight there afterward. It’s a gorgeous park—easily top three among all the parks I’ve been to. It also made me realize I haven’t spent spring in northern China in years. The weather was perfect: not too hot, not too cold.</p>

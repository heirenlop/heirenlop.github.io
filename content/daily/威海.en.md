            <h2>Introduction</h2>
            <p>2019-06-20---2021-07-10</p>
            <p>I came to Weihai for grad school and lived here for two years. It’s a great place to settle down: mountains, the sea, blue skies, great food, an airport, a port, fewer crowds, and genuinely nice people. The wind, though, is seriously intense.
            </p>
            <p>2026-05-01</p>
            <p>A few years after graduation, I came back to Weihai with my girlfriend to attend her best friend’s wedding.</p>
        </section>

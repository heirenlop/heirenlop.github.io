Introduction

2019-06-20 --- 2021-07-10

I came to Weihai for my graduate studies and stayed for two years. This place is very suitable for settling down. It has mountains, seas, blue skies, delicious food, an airport, and a port. The population is small, and the people are nice. The only downside is the strong wind.

1. Huanhai Road

2019-09-10

On the left side of Huanhai Road, there are mountains, and on the right, there is the sea. It's very beautiful.

2. Rongcheng

2019-09-20

During the Mid-Autumn Festival, I rented a car with a friend, went to Rongcheng, then Qingdao, Huangdao, and finally returned to Weihai. Rongcheng is even cleaner than Weihai, and the roads are wider. Unfortunately, the weather was bad during the two days we visited.

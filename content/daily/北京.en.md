        <h2>5. Yangtai Mountain</h2>
            <p>2026-02-23 <i class="fas fa-sun"></i></p>
            <p>Yangtai Mountain, Fenghuangling, Jiufeng, and Miaofeng Mountain are all in the same area, in the northwest corner of Haidian District. Their summit elevations are all around 1,200 meters. From Yangtai Mountain, you can keep hiking all the way to Jiufeng or Miaofeng Mountain, but it’s a very long route. I didn’t go that far this time—I only covered about half the route, then turned back and hiked down after reaching Miao’erwa Tea Shed.</p>
